The mission

Let's build an AI data center
we own!

A global, peer-to-peer network for local AI — built by the community, for the community.

01  Own the infrastructure

Let's own the infrastructure we use.

  • Let's build a decentralized, global, peer-to-peer data center — especially for local AI.
  • Let's own our infrastructure, so we can use it from the community, for the community.
  • Let's be independent from Big Tech and the clouds.
  • Let's be independent from models being made inaccessible.
  • Let's be independent from subscriptions and rate limits we can't influence.
  • Let's be self-determined.
02  Available for everyone

Let's make local AI available for all of us.

  • Use models that others offer that your own machine can't run — someone out there can.
  • Not everyone can afford a machine that runs local AI — through the network, everyone can use it anyway.
  • Offer some when you can, use some when you need — together we keep local AI within everyone's reach.
  • No-GPU users will be able to buy credits to use the network's resources (coming) — the money goes to the people providing resources, and part is pooled and donated to open-source projects, decided by LAPSUS users.
03  Support open source

Let's make AI available for small & open-source projects.

The idea: donate resources and credits to open-source projects so they can use the network's AI for their own needs — not for heavy software development (that would eat too much), but for everyday workloads like translations or text summaries.

  • Small and open-source projects get AI compute they couldn't otherwise afford.

Still an idea — the exact mechanics are being figured out. Answers will come.

Let's be free, and self-determined.
