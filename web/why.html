The name

Why “LAPSUS”?

An acronym, a word, and a pun — all three fit.

1 · An acronym

Local AI · P2P · Social · Users · System — a network of people running local AI for one another.

2 · A word

Lapsus is Latin for a slip (as in lapsus linguae, a slip of the tongue) — a small, deliberate slip out of Big Tech's grip, back onto our own machines.

3 · A pun

Say it “LAB-sus” — lab's us. The network is our own lab: a decentralized AI data center owned by the people running it.

Our lab. Our infrastructure. Our AI.
